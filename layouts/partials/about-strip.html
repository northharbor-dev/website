About

Built by an engineer, for engineers.

NorthHarbor was started by Bob Hong — an enterprise architect who got tired of watching capable AI agents step on each other's work. The tools to build with AI exist. The coordination layer didn't.

Every product in the NorthHarbor family is built using the same infrastructure it describes. Waypoint coordinates the build of Breezy. Pilot will manage its own development. We don't ship things we don't use ourselves.
